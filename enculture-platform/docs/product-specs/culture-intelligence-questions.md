# Culture Intelligence — Enzo Question Set

## Culture Intelligence discovery / conversation flow
1. `What does our current culture profile look like?`
2. `How would you describe the culture strengths in my team?`
3. `What patterns are emerging from the culture survey responses?`
4. `Which culture dimensions seem strongest and which need attention?`
5. `What are the most important signals from our open-text feedback?`
6. `Can you help me understand the trust / collaboration / innovation trends?`
7. `What stands out about our team’s culture compared to the broader organization?`

## Insight analysis / interpretation
8. `What do you notice about the gap between how people feel and how they act?`
9. `What might be causing the lower scores in collaboration?`
10. `What themes do you see in the comments about leadership?`
11. `Where are the biggest opportunity areas for culture improvement?`
12. `What does the data suggest about our psychological safety?`
13. `What is the story behind our innovation dimension score?`

## Action planning / intervention design
14. `Given these culture insights, what should we focus on first?`
15. `What actions might improve our team’s trust and transparency?`
16. `How should we turn these findings into a practical culture intervention?`
17. `What would success look like if we improved this culture dimension?`
18. `What are the likely obstacles to improving our culture here?`
19. `What could be a good first step for our next culture initiative?`



> Note: For Culture Intelligence, the focus is on turning abstract culture data into measurable insights, not on giving soft advice. These prompts should test whether Enzo can surface patterns, interpret culture dimensions, and help build concrete action without making unsupported judgments.
