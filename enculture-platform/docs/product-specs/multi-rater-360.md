# Multi-Rater 360 Feedback — Product Spec

> Status: Draft — Needs input from Product Head and Tech Team

## Vision

Go beyond traditional 360 feedback collection to deliver AI-enhanced insight generation — helping individuals and organizations understand not just what feedback says, but what it means and what to do about it.

## Target Users

| Persona | Role | Key Need |
|---------|------|----------|
| HR Team | Program administrators | Easy 360 setup, tracking, and reporting |
| Coach | Development partner | Rich data to guide coaching conversations |
| Manager | Feedback recipient & giver | Actionable insights about their leadership |
| Employee | Feedback recipient & giver | Fair process with meaningful development output |
| Executive Sponsor | Program owner | ROI and aggregate impact data |

## Core Features (To Be Detailed)

### Phase 1 — Foundation
- [ ] 360 survey builder (custom questionnaires per organization)
- [ ] Rater nomination and management workflow
- [ ] Multi-channel feedback collection (email, app, link)
- [ ] Individual 360 feedback reports
- [ ] Manager / HR dashboard for tracking completion

### Phase 2 — Intelligence
- [ ] AI-powered theme extraction from open-text feedback
- [ ] Blind spot analysis (self-rating vs. others' ratings)
- [ ] Sentiment and tone analysis of written feedback
- [ ] Strength and development area identification
- [ ] AI-generated coaching prompts and development suggestions

### Phase 3 — Ecosystem
- [ ] Integration with Competency Assessment (map 360 to competencies)
- [ ] Integration with Culture Intelligence (team culture + individual feedback)
- [ ] Coach matching and assignment
- [ ] Progress tracking (compare 360 results over time)
- [ ] Group/team-level aggregate reports

## Rater Categories

Standard 360 rater groups:
1. **Self** — The individual being assessed
2. **Manager** — Direct supervisor
3. **Peers** — Colleagues at the same level
4. **Direct Reports** — People who report to the individual
5. **Others / Stakeholders** — Cross-functional partners, clients, mentors

## Open Questions

- What's our confidentiality model? (Anonymous? Confidential with attribution to category?)
- Minimum number of raters per category for anonymity?
- How do we handle situations where feedback is potentially harmful or biased?
- What AI guardrails prevent the system from surfacing unfair conclusions?
- Report format — PDF? Interactive dashboard? Both?
